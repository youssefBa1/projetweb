My Experience at IIT

A balanced perspective on my university journey

About My Journey

As a student at the Institut des Ingénieurs de la Technologie (IIT), I've had the opportunity to develop my technical skills and grow both academically and personally. Here's an honest reflection on my experience.

What I Like ✅

  • Quality Education: The curriculum is well-structured and covers modern technologies. I've learned practical skills in web development, programming, and software engineering that are directly applicable to real-world projects.
  • Hands-On Projects: IIT emphasizes practical learning through projects and internships. This approach has helped me build a solid portfolio and gain confidence in applying theoretical knowledge to actual development work.
  • Supportive Professors: Most professors are knowledgeable and willing to help students understand complex concepts. Their industry experience brings valuable insights into the classroom.
  • Career Preparation: The institution provides internship opportunities and focuses on employability skills. This has helped me prepare for the professional world and understand industry expectations.

What Needs Improvement ⚠️

  • Too Many Subjects Per Semester: The workload is overwhelming with too many subjects crammed into one semester. It's hard to manage everything and the schedule feels packed, leaving little time to properly understand each course.
  • Focus on Variety Instead of Mastery: The curriculum covers a lot of different topics superficially rather than going deep into any one area. I would prefer to master a few technologies really well instead of learning many things at a surface level.
  • Repeated Annoying Subjects: Some subjects feel redundant and get repeated across different semesters with slightly different names. It's frustrating to sit through similar content again when that time could be spent learning something new and useful.
  • Administrative Delays: Sometimes administrative processes can be slow, whether it's getting documents, approvals, or responses to queries. Better organization would help students focus more on their studies.

Overall Thoughts

Despite some challenges, my time at IIT has been valuable for my professional development. I've gained technical skills, completed meaningful projects, and built a foundation for my career. Like any institution, there's room for improvement, but the education and opportunities provided have prepared me well for the tech industry.

Overall Rating: ⭐⭐⭐⭐☆ 7/10
